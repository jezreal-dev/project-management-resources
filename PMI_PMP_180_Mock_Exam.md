# PMI PMP 180-Question Mock Examination

This is a full-scale practice examination modeled after the official PMI Project Management Professional (PMP)® exam content outline. It consists of 180 situational questions divided by domain, complete with an answer key and justifications at the end.

## Exam Guidelines:
* **Total Questions:** 180
* **Time Limit:** 230 minutes (3 hours and 50 minutes)
* **Structure:** Domain I: People (Questions 1-76), Domain II: Process (Questions 77-166), Domain III: Business Environment (Questions 167-180)

---

## Domain I: People (Questions 1-76)

#### Question 1
A key team member has started submitting deliverables late. When confronted, they explain that they are feeling overwhelmed by the competing demands of two different project managers in a matrix organization. What is the first action the project manager should take?
* A. Escalate the issue to the sponsor to reassign the resource.
* B. Instruct the team member to prioritize your project over the other.
* C. Schedule a meeting with the other project manager to discuss resource sharing and coordinate the team member's workload.
* D. Report the team member to the HR department for poor performance.
