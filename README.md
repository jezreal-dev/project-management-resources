This repository contains a comprehensive collection of study resources, visual guides, templates, and exam keys designed to prepare candidates for both local University of Ibadan Project Management courses (in collaboration with ARP Technology) and the official PMI Project Management Professional (PMP)® certification.

## Repository Contents

### 1. [PMBOK Comprehensive Study Guide](PMBOK_Comprehensive_Study_Guide.md)
The primary reference manual built to align with PMBOK Guide 6th and 7th Editions.
* **Theory & Standards:** Covers 20 core chapters including general PM concepts, 12 Delivery Principles, 8 Performance Domains, change control, quality, risk, and Agile/Scrum.
* **12 Embedded Mermaid Diagrams:** Visual flowcharts and hierarchies representing the project lifecycle, organizational structures, change control workflows, PDM network dependencies, cost baselines, the Tuckman team ladder, the contract risk spectrum, the stakeholder grid, the Scrum loop, and quality tools.
* **Document Templates:** Formatted layout tables for Project Charters, Stakeholder Registers, Risk Registers, Change Logs, Issue Logs, and Communication Plans.
* **High-Yield Practice Questions:** 10 situational multiple-choice mock questions with correct answers and justifications.

### 2. [PMI PMP 180-Question Mock Examination](PMI_PMP_180_Mock_Exam.md)
A full-scale practice examination modeled after the official PMP exam content outline.
* **Structure:** 180 situational multiple-choice questions.
* **Domain Distribution:**
  * **Domain I: People:** 76 Questions (conflict management, matrix environments, team safety, and coaching).
  * **Domain II: Process:** 90 Questions (EVM math, change control rules, contracting strategies, and root-cause analysis).
  * **Domain III: Business Environment:** 14 Questions (regulatory compliance, strategic business case alignment, and system transitions).
* **Answer Key:** Detailed justifications and rationales for all 180 questions located at the end of the file.

### 3. [Solved March 2026 Final Examination](Solved_March_2026_Final_Examination.md)
The official solved key for the final examination conducted by ARP Technology in collaboration with the University of Ibadan.
* **Section A:** 40 multiple-choice questions with correct options and PMBOK-aligned justifications.
* **Section B:** 6 core project management theory essay answers (defining PM, WBS, stakeholder engagement, EVM terms, risk strategies, and Scrum roles).
* **Section C:** 3 comprehensive scenario-based case study solutions (planning a community ICT training center, recovering a failing mobile application project, and implementing Agile Scrum for online banking software).

## How to Study

1. **Review the Core Guide:** Open `PMBOK_Comprehensive_Study_Guide.md` to study the 20 theoretical chapters. Focus on the vector Mermaid diagrams to visualize process flows.
2. **Practice the Local Exam:** Test your knowledge against `Solved_March_2026_Final_Examination.md` to see the structure and expected grading standards for UI/ARP course exams.
3. **Simulate the PMP Mock Exam:** Schedule 230 uninterrupted minutes to take the practice test in `PMI_PMP_180_Mock_Exam.md`. Grade yourself using the Answer Key at the bottom of the file, analyzing the justifications for any incorrect answers.
